Nafra2 service iteration Version 15.0

Seventh iteration of service with Nafra2 data Tested

Version 15 focusses on depth

V14 research findings

  • Depth is useful (provides context), but is not in the right place for users
  • This part of a bigger issue about where information sits on the service. This came out more this round from users with neurodiverse needs
  • We need time to look at the full user journey, and where depth sits Participants struggled without explanatory information about what the ratings mean on the depth page
  • As we've seen before, the stats continue to cause confusion
  • Depth designs do not work for mobile
  • 2050s dropdown caused significant confusion and mistrust of the data overall. We need to explore this issue further. There is a serious risk of reputational damage to the EA and the government
  • As we've seen before, people do not think about 'surface water' flooding and 'rivers and sea' flooding. It's all just 'flooding'
