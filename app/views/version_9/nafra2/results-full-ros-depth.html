How deep any flood water could be

At the moment, this area has a:

  • high chance (more than 3.3% chance) of flooding up to 15cm
  • medium chance (1% to 3.3% chance) of flooding up to 30cm
  • low chance (less than 1% chance) of flooding up to 60cm

Flood protection measures are usually effective up to 60cm.

Read about how you can adapt your home or business to the risk of flooding.

60cm (2ft)
Low 0.1% to 1% chance of flooding up to 60cm
What can happen if flood water reaches 60cm

At 60cm, flood water can:

  • get into some homes and buildings
  • float most vehicles, included 4x4s
30cm (1ft)
Medium 1% to 3.3% chance of flooding up to 30cm
What can happen if flood water reaches 30cm

At 30cm, flood water can:

  • get into some homes and buildings
  • move a car
  • damage roads and cause major traffic disruption
15cm (6in)
High More than 3.3% chance of flooding up to 15cm

At 15cm, flood water can:

  • get into some homes and buildings, especially those with basements
  • block roads
  • damage your car or cause a breakdown

How climate change could affect the depth of any flood water

The latest climate projections show that by 2050, this area will have a:

  • high chance of flooding up to 30cm
  • medium chance of flooding up to 60cm
  • low chance of flooding up to 90cm
90cm (3ft)
Low 0.1% to 1% chance of flooding up to 90cm
What can happen if flood water reaches 90cm

You should not try and keep flood water out of buildings if it's 90cm or more. It can cause collapse or permanent structural damage.
