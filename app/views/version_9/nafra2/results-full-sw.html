What this information tells you
We use the best data available to estimate the surface water flood risk for an area.

We cannot tell you whether an individual property will definitely flood.

Neighbouring houses in a street may have different levels of flood risk. This could be because one is on higher land or has higher floor levels.

Predicting flood risk is not an exact science - flood events of any size can happen anywhere at any time.
What makes an area more likely to flood from surface water
Surface water flooding is sometimes known as flash flooding. It happens when rainwater cannot drain away through normal drainage systems. It's a problem because:
  • it often happens in places you don't expect, like up hills
  • it's hard to predict, as heavy rain sometimes only affects a small part of an area
  • where there's lots of tarmac and concrete the water has nowhere to go
