Service iterations Version 7.0 & 7.1

Exploring a new way of displaying content on the summary page by displaying only the essentials and iterating on advanced options in the previous map version.

Insights from prior user research

Advanced toggle in the menu was causing some issues for users after toggling it back on, they have to scroll to the botton of menu to untoggle. It slso wasn’t quite a natural interaction to toggle something and the content appears above the element the user has just pressed.

It was difficult to find appropriate language that matched what the toggle was doing, ‘add technical layers’ would be correct for adding the velocity option to the map, but not for adding different flood risk types (reservoirs and rivers and the sea), so adding a ‘go to advanced map’ button would tell the user accurately what is happening.

After seeing that citizen users don’t typically use turn off layers, in order to reduce cognitive load removing the scenario controls for extent should help them focus on understanding the key and what they see on the map without more elements on the screen to understand or interact with Professionals will likely still use the toggle display layers, and it is more appropriately placed under the advanced options section.
