Check the long term flood risk for an area in England

Use this service to find out:

  • the long term flood risk for an area in England
  • the possible causes of flooding
  • how to manage flood risk

This service tells you about an area’s long term risk from:

  • rivers and the sea
  • surface water
  • reservoirs
  • some ground water

Check the flood risk in Scotland, flood risk in Wales or flood risk in Northern Ireland

Before you start

This service does not tell you:

  • how likely it is that an individual property will flood
  • about flood risk from sources such as blocked drains and burst pipes
