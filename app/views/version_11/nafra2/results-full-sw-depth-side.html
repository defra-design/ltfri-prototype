What can happen if flooding reaches different levels

At 20cm, flood water can:

  • get into some homes and buildings, especially those with basements
  • block roads
  • damage your car or cause a breakdown

At 30cm, flood water can:

  • get into some homes and buildings
  • move a car
  • damage roads and cause major traffic disruption

At 60cm, flood water can:

  • get into some homes and buildings
  • float most vehicles, including 4x4s

You should not try and keep flood water out of buildings if it's 90cm or more. It can cause collapse or permanent structural damage.

What you can do

Flood protection measures are usually effective up to 60cm. Read about how you can adapt your home or business to the risk of flooding.
