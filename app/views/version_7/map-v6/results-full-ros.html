What this information tells you
We use the best data available to estimate the rivers and sea flood risk for an area.

We cannot tell you whether an individual property will definitely flood.

The information takes into account any flood defences. They can help reduce the chance of flooding but cannot completely prevent it because:
  • they can fail
  • water could spill over the top if it is deep enough
Neighbouring houses in a street may have different levels of flood risk. This could be because one is on higher land or has higher floor levels.

Predicting flood risk is not an exact science - flood events of any size can happen anywhere at any time.
What makes an area more likely to flood from rivers and the sea
Low-lying areas that are close to rivers or the sea are more likely to flood when water levels rise.
