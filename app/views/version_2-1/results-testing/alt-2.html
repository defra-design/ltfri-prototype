Flood risk summary for the area around:

27A, HOLME LANE, SHEFFIELD, S6 4JP

Surface water

High risk

What this information means

Surface water happens when rainwater does not drain away through the normal drainage systems but lies on or flows over the ground instead.

This flood risk summary reports the highest risk from surface water within a 20-metre radius of this property.

High risk means this area has a chance of flooding of greater than 3.3% each year.
