Version 19 focuses on testing the new content for groundwater, including the language used for the binary risk ratings 'Outside a flood alert area' and 'inside a flood alaert area'.

V19.0 changes

  • The pages that will be tested are the main risk result page and the 'Groundwater: Understand your flood risk' page.

  • We have also introducted the start of the 'Reservoirs: Understanding your flood risk' - Due to the groundwater and reservoir pages now being separated, we needed to add a page for reservoir content, should any users click any reservoir realted links.

  • Content pages have also been updated to match the liver service more closely, for exmaple, the content list to navigate to other pages, was situated in the navigation, this doesn't need to be placed here, as this rule only generally applies if there are links to a a new action, as per GDS guidelines, so it has been replaced by the standard GOV UK contents list.

  • The boxes used to show the 'risk result' for outside or inside a flood risk areas are currently grey. A decision is needed (following user testing) as to whether these ramain grey, or if users expect there to be a colour convension, similar to those used in the other risk rating (eg. red, ornage and green)
