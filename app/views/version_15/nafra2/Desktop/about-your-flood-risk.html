About your flood risk

Five million homes and businesses in England are at risk of flooding.

Flooding can come from a range of sources.

An area can be at risk even if it has not flooded before.

Our flood risk data does not include every feature like kerbs, or fences and walls around a building. That means we can only tell you the flood risk of the land around a building, not the building itself.
