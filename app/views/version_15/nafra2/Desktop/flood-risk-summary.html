Why we cannot tell you the flood risk of a specific property
Our flood risk data does not include every feature like kerbs, or fences and walls around a building. That means we can only tell you the flood risk of the land around a building, not the building itself.
Neighbouring houses may have different flood risk ratings. This could be because one is on higher land or nearer water, for example.

About surface water flood risk and climate change

Our surface water flood risk data covers the period from 2040 to 2060.

Climate change is increasing the chances of flooding. Climate models project we will see warmer and wetter winters, and more frequent and intense weather events.

But the models cannot predict exactly when the chance of flooding from surface water will go up.

Any increase in flood risk could happen at any time during this period.
