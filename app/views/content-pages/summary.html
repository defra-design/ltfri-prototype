How often could it flood
High chance of flooding means there could be a flood from surface water more than 3 or 4 times in a 100-year period.

The map shows:
  • how widespread any flooding could be
  • an estimation of where any flood water might spread to
How deep any flooding could be
If an area floods, the flood water is most likely to reach a maximum of 30cm deep.

There are 2 other possible scenarios that could affect an area:
  • Flood water of up to 90cm deep. This type of flooding is less likely but possible.
  • Flood water of more than 90cm. Flooding of this severity is very unlikely.
